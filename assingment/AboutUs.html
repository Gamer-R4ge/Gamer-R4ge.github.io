About Us






What we do


Grace Gamers is a team of students from Grace Lutheran
College who play competitive games against other teams.
The team of students play a competetitive game called Rocket League.
Rocket league is a game in which the players use cars
to play soccer in teams of 1,2 or 3 players




Club Goals


Our team of gamers have set their goals for this year.
The students are planning on competing in regional
tournaments, and are striving for first place.
The team also hopes to be able to recruit more
students to create a 2nd team for competing.
